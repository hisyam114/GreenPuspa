# Green Puspa Website Design Specification

## Project Name
**Green Puspa** *Kampung Urban Farming RW 06 Utan Kayu Selatan*

## Project Vision
Create a modern, inspiring, community-centered website that showcases how Green Puspa transformed a dense urban neighborhood into a productive, green, and sustainable environment.

**The experience should feel:**
* Warm
* Human
* Community-driven
* Educational
* Professional
* Trustworthy
* Nature-inspired

**Visitors should immediately understand:**
* What Green Puspa is
* Why it exists
* How it benefits the community
* What activities take place there
* Why it is an example of successful urban farming

## Design Personality
**Primary Traits:**
* Friendly
* Green
* Sustainable
* Community-focused
* Modern
* Educational

**Avoid:**
* Corporate appearance
* Government portal appearance
* Dashboard appearance
* Industrial appearance
* Technical IoT interfaces

**The website should feel more like:**
* National Geographic Storytelling
* Apple Environmental Pages
* UN Sustainability Projects
* Modern NGO Websites

## Visual Theme
**Theme Name:** Urban Nature  
**Concept:** The meeting point between:
* Dense city life
* Community collaboration
* Green spaces
* Sustainable food production

**Visual storytelling should emphasize:**
* Plants
* Community activities
* Gardens
* Hydroponics
* Learning
* Harvesting
* Environmental improvement

## Color Palette
* **Primary Green:** `#2E7D32`
* **Secondary Green:** `#66BB6A`
* **Light Green:** `#A5D6A7`
* **Background:** `#F8FFF8`
* **Dark Text:** `#1B1B1B`
* **Light Gray:** `#F5F5F5`
* **White:** `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
**Headings:** Poppins
* *Characteristics:* Friendly, Modern, Strong, Clean

**Body Text:** Inter
* *Characteristics:* Highly readable, Modern, Neutral

## Layout Principles
**Use:**
* Spacious layout
* Large imagery
* Clear hierarchy
* Strong storytelling

*The design should feel breathable. Avoid cramped sections. Use generous whitespace.*

## Website Structure

### Home Page
#### Hero Section
* Full-width hero image.
* **Show:** Urban farming activities, Community members caring for plants, Hydroponic installations.
* **Large headline:** Green Puspa - Kampung Urban Farming RW 06 Utan Kayu Selatan
* **Subheadline:** Mewujudkan lingkungan hijau, sehat, dan produktif melalui semangat gotong royong.
* **Buttons:** Tentang Kami, Jelajahi Program
* *Hero should feel inspiring and hopeful.*

#### Statistics Section
Four large cards using animated counters:
* **2017:** Program Dimulai
* **1500+:** Lubang Hidroponik
* **RW 06:** Lokasi Komunitas
* **100%:** Gotong Royong Warga

#### About Preview
Two-column layout:
* **Left:** Large image.
* **Right:** Community story summary.
* **Button:** Pelajari Lebih Lanjut

#### Program Highlights
Card Grid (4 cards). Each card includes Illustration, Title, Description, and Learn More link:
* Hydroponik
* Akuaponik
* Edukasi Lingkungan
* Ketahanan Pangan

#### Community Impact
Large visual section showing Before and After transformation concepts. Include:
* Environment
* Education
* Community
* Sustainability
* *Use infographic-style presentation.*

#### Timeline Preview
Horizontal timeline with milestones (2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024+) and interactive hover states.

#### Gallery Preview
Masonry photo layout with mixed image sizes and hover animations.
* **Button:** Lihat Semua Galeri

#### Partners Section
Logo grid. Use grayscale logos (color appears on hover).
* *Examples:* PLN, Community partners, Government institutions

#### Final CTA
Large green section.
* **Headline:** Mari Mengenal Green Puspa Lebih Dekat
* **Buttons:** Hubungi Kami, Kunjungi Program

### About Page
* **Hero:** Large banner image. Headline: Tentang Green Puspa
* **Story Section:** Narrative layout focusing on Origins, Community initiative, Environmental transformation. Use timeline storytelling.
* **Vision & Mission:** Two-column cards. Modern clean design.
* **Philosophy:** Quote-style section. Highlight: *"Dari Warga, Untuk Warga, dan Oleh Warga"*

### Programs Page
Display program cards where each program opens a detailed section.
* **Hydroponics:** Large visual, Benefits grid, Process illustration.
* **Aquaponics:** Educational diagrams, Plant + fish ecosystem visualization.
* **Environmental Greening:** Urban green corridor concept, Before-after imagery.
* **Community Education:** Workshop visuals, Community learning imagery.

### Smart Urban Farming Page
**Important Design Rule:** This page is educational only.
* **Do NOT create:** Dashboards, Charts, Monitoring panels, Control screens, Device interfaces.
* **Instead show:** Illustrations, Diagrams, Educational content.
* *Focus on understanding concepts.*

### Timeline Page
Full interactive vertical timeline with year markers, expandable events, and modern storytelling layout.

### Gallery Page
Pinterest-style masonry layout with a Lightbox modal and smooth transitions.
* **Filters:** Hydroponics, Aquaponics, Community, Events, Harvest, Visitors

### News Page
Magazine-style layout. Featured article at the top, grid articles below. Large imagery, readable typography.

### FAQ Page
Accordion layout. Friendly visual style with simple interactions.

### Contact Page
Split layout.
* **Left:** Contact details.
* **Right:** Map, Contact form, Social media icons.

### Footer
Four columns:
* About
* Programs
* Resources
* Contact
* **Bottom section:** Copyright, Social Links, Privacy Policy

## Imagery Direction
**Photography should emphasize:**
* Real community members
* Plants
* Hydroponics
* Harvesting
* Green pathways
* Learning activities

**Avoid:**
* Stock corporate imagery
* Technology-focused imagery
* Server rooms
* Dashboards
* Generic office scenes

## Illustration Style
* Modern flat illustration.
* Nature-focused.
* Friendly.
* Soft gradients.
* Rounded shapes.
* Organic forms.

## Motion Design
*Subtle animations only.*
* **Use:** Fade-in, Slide-up, Counter animation, Hover effects.
* **Avoid:** Excessive motion, Parallax overload, Flashy transitions.

## Mobile Experience
Mobile-first approach. All sections must:
* Stack vertically
* Maintain readability
* Preserve image quality
* Keep touch targets large

**Navigation:** Hamburger menu, Sticky header, Smooth scrolling.

## Accessibility
Design must support:
* High contrast text
* Large touch targets
* Keyboard navigation
* Screen reader compatibility

## Desired User Feeling
When a visitor leaves the website, they should feel:
* Inspired
* Educated
* Connected to the community
* Interested in urban farming
* Optimistic about sustainable cities

*The website should communicate that Green Puspa is not simply a farming project, but a successful example of how community collaboration can transform an urban neighborhood into a greener, healthier, and more productive place.*
